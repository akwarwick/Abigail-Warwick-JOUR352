Social media use in the United States

How many adults used social media from 2012-2021 and which platforms are most common?

By Abigail Warwick

Social media has grown immensely in the past 20 years in both users and platforms. As technology becomes more accessible, more people have been able to join platforms or even make new ones themselves. These charts show the growth of social media users and platforms, broken down for the last nine years. As time goes on, there are more users and more platforms.
